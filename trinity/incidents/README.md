# Trinity Incident Logs

Incidents are logged, not optimized.
They are remembered, not corrected.

Purpose:
This directory contains append-only records of system failures,
diagnostics, interventions, and outcomes.

These logs preserve reasoning under pressure.

---

## Invariant (Append-Only)

Incidents are logged, not optimized.  
They are remembered, not corrected.

No incident record may be rewritten to improve outcomes.
Only additional witness entries may be appended.
